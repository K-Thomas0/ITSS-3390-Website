About the Baker

My name is Khurstyn, and I’m someone who’s always looking for ways to make life a little easier and more enjoyable. I love cooking, working on crocheting projects, and learning new skills especially when it comes to design and technology. As a college student myself, I struggled with finding quick, affordable meals without having to leave campus or rely on the dining hall, which was always inconvenient. That experience inspired me to create my website: a place filled with simple, budget-friendly recipes that can be made right in a dorm room using only a microwave or fridge. It’s my way of helping other students save time, money, and stress while still enjoying good food.
